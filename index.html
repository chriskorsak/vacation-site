Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia

The place to go if you have a month to spare!

The place I'd like to go most of all is North Macedonia. You might know this country as plain-old "Macedonia," but they were recently forced to change their name by Greece, who used to refer to them as the "Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia." Don't get any ideas about Alexander the Great not being from Greece!
